81. Oak Bay Recreation – Memberships & Pricing
What: Time-based passes (1-month, 3-month, annual, or regional annual) and single-admission/visit passes that unlock unlimited drop-in access to the fitness centre, group fitness, swimming, skating, and most sport sessions (excluding golf and tennis). Passes start the day you buy them, are non-transferable/non-refundable, and have some activity-specific restrictions.
Where: Oak Bay Recreation Centre & Henderson Recreation Centre (plus Monterey group fitness access with a pass)
When: Passes valid during regular operating hours; see Drop-in Schedules for class times
Cost: Rates effective Sept 1, 2025 – Aug 31, 2026. Time-based: Adult 19+ $75 (1 mo) / $168.75 (3 mo) / $465 (annual) / $570 (regional annual); Adult 60+ or Student 19+ $60 / $135 / $465 / $570; Child/Youth 6-18 $42.50 / $95.75 / $255.75; Family annual $930. Single admissions: Adult 19+ $7.50 (single) / $67.50 (10x) / $150 (25x); Adult 60+ or Student 19+ $6 / $54 / $120; Child/Youth 6-18 $4.25 / $38.25 / $85; Family $15 / $135 / $300 with extra child $1.50. Visit passes expire 2 years after purchase.
Notes: Memberships cover both Oak Bay Recreation Centre and Henderson Recreation Centre drop-ins; Monterey Recreation Centre group fitness classes are included with an active pass.

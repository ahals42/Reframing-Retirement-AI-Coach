Reframing Retirement Coach

What It Does: Supports everyday physical activity by helping you reflect on what works for you, stay motivated, and adapt activity in ways that fit your life. It can also help you explore local opportunities to be active and connect you with relevant information or resources within the Reframing Retirement module.

What It Does Not Do: It is not intended to replace a medical professional and does not provide medical advice, diagnoses, or risk assessments. For medical questions or urgent health concerns, users should consult a qualified healthcare professional.
